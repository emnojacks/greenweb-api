Green Domain Search

Did you know that every website and host has a carbon footprint?

As ever more of the global population crosses the digital divide, spending their work and leisure hours online, the impact of our online world expands. Like most environmental issues, energy inefficiency and waste are hard to see, even moreso when it's dematerialized in bits and bytes.

However, metrics have been developed recently to rate the carbon impact and energy sources of websites and their hosts. Green Web Foundation has collected a robust, opensource database of these resources and they are here now at your fingertips.

Enter the website url you would like to check and our API will return if the webhost and website are truly green.
